Frequently Asked Questions

Want to learn more? Email Jake at jake@terabytegames.com

Where is camp located?

Camp is located in a computer Lab at 4444 Gloster Rd, Dallas, Tx, 75220. The lab contains state of the art computers, and campers each get their own computer.

Are there any prerequisities?

For the beginning classes, the only prerequisites are basic computer skills such as using the mouse or keyboard, navigating in windows, and saving and finding files. The Advanced courses are just continuations of the beginning ones, so only the beginning class is required.

What ages is the camp for?

Camp is for students entering grades 3 to 8.

Breaks and Lunch?

We have just enough break time to get the students outside to play. We have a basketball court, ping pong table, board games, etc. for campers to play with. Campers need to bring their own lunch and can also bring snacks if they want. We have refrigerators and microwaves for camper use if they need them. We also have water for everyone, but bringing a water bottle is recommended. It can get very hot. If the temperature is too high, we will shorten break and go back inside.
